PTL Response Guide — Post Video

Principle: Answer only what's asked। Don't explain full system। Default: "पूरी जानकारी app में मिलेगी। इस महीने सब पुराने तरीके से।"

What We're Ultimately Measuring

Leading indicators (Days 1-5): Did they see it? Did they complete it? Did they pass the quiz?
→ Contextual seen %, Blocker %, Quiz %

Lagging indicator (Post-launch): Did they understand it?
→ PTL call volume in week 1. If education worked, partners self-serve from the app. If it failed, they call.

The real test: When a partner sees their first bonus breakdown in the new app, do they understand it — or do they call PTL asking "yeh kya hai?"

Survey Questions (5 questions, simple recall)

Q1: Wiom system में कुछ updates आ रहे हैं — आपको पता है?
Tests: basic awareness. Yes/No.

Q2: बोनस के बारे में क्या बदल रहा है — बता सकते हैं?
Tests: earning recall. Looking for "internet quality" or "₹120". Open-ended — don't prompt.

Q3: NetBox के बारे में कुछ नया सुना? क्या याद है?
Tests: NetBox recall. Looking for "₹50", "15 din", "₹2". Open-ended.

Q4: Connections अब कैसे मिलेंगी?
Tests: work allocation recall. Looking for "system se". Open-ended.

Q5: इस महीने कुछ बदलेगा या नहीं?
Tests: protection recall. Correct answer: "नहीं, पुराने तरीके से चलेगा।"
