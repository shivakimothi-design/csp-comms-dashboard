CSP Migration — Communication Plan

05/04/26 → 10/04/26  |  State-based, behaviour-triggered  |  OWNER role only

Objective

By 10/04/26 (app live): Every partner OWNER has a mental model of the new system — no surprises when they open the new app.
By 30/04/26 (month end): Every partner trusts the system — no unexplained number, no surprise deduction, no confusion call to Trust Line.

Measurable:

  1. 100% of OWNERs complete the education blocker before 10/04
  2. >70% complete the quiz — confirmed comprehension, not just viewed
  3. Trust Line call volume does not spike in week 1 post-launch

Constraints

ConstraintImplication
5 days only05/04 to 09/04. App goes live 10/04. Every education touchpoint must land within this window.
Old app: static content onlyPopups can show text/visuals/video. Cannot show CSP-specific data (no device counts, no bonus history). Same for all 1,200 OWNERs.
Asset/NetBox page has no dataCannot leverage this page for contextual education. NetBox education goes through other channels.
OWNER role only~1,400-1,460 unique OWNERs open the app daily. Technicians/ADMINs excluded.
No field support / BDO teamsAll education must work without human mediation.
WA/SMS = redirect onlyNever carries education. Gets partner to the app where education lives.
