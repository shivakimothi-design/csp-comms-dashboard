Day-by-Day — What Happens at Each State

1
05/04

Contextual First Touch — Meet Them Where They Are

Partner opens app for daily work → sees a teaser in whatever section they visit

What happens: All section popups are planted. Whichever section the partner (OWNER only, not technicians) visits first — that popup fires.

Confirmed priority order (by visit frequency):
#1 Dashboard (most visited — landing page) → #2 Wallet (money check) → #3 Tickets (work)

This means most partners will first see the Dashboard popup. Those who go straight to Wallet or Tickets will see the section-specific education first. Either way, they're primed for the blocker on Day 2.
PrioritySectionPopup content (connected to system reboot narrative)
#1 Dashboard Wiom ka system badal raha hai.
Kaam, kamai, devices, aur customer plans — sab mein badlaav aa raha hai. Wallet aur Tickets mein jaake details dekhein. Puri picture kal milegi.
#2 Wallet Wiom ka system badal raha hai — aur aapki kamai ka tarika bhi.
₹300/connection same. Bonus ab monthly, quality basis, up to ₹120. Puri picture kal milegi.
#3 Tickets Wiom ka system badal raha hai — aur kaam ka tarika bhi.
Jo ticket solve kar rahe hain — naye system mein yeh quality count hogi. Connections system se aayengi. Puri picture kal milegi.
— Earnings Wiom ka system badal raha hai — earning ka structure bhi naya hoga.
₹300 same. Bonus up to ₹120/month, 5 quality factors. Puri picture kal milegi.
— Asset / NetBox Wiom ka system badal raha hai — devices ke rules bhi.
15+ din idle = ₹2/day. Return = ₹50. ₹200 deposit — sahi return par wapas. Details naye app mein. Puri picture kal milegi.
Every popup ends with "puri picture kal milegi" — this primes the partner for the blocker coming on Day 2. The contextual popup is the appetiser. The blocker is the main course.

Note: This applies to Partners (OWNER role) only. Technicians/ADMINs are excluded from the migration education flow.
State transition: S0 → S1
WA (same day): "Wiom app kholein — zaruri update hai" — sent to all. Redirect only.
2
06/04

BLOCKER — The Full Picture (All Partners)

Cannot use the app until they complete this. The one forced education in the entire migration.

What happens: Every partner who opens the app on 06/04 (or any day after) hits the blocker. Cannot dismiss. Cannot access tickets, wallet, anything. Must go through all cards and tap "samajh gaya."

Blocker content — 3 cards + video + "samajh gaya":

Card 1 — Work: Connections ab system se aayengi. Mandatory. No manual process. Jitna accha kaam, utna zyada milega. App zaruri hai.

Card 2 — Earning: ₹300/connection same. Bonus ab ek monthly amount — up to ₹120. Quality pe depend: uptime, speed, resolution, complaints, installation quality. NetBox: ₹2/day idle fee, ₹50 collection, ₹200 deposit (wapas on proper return).

Card 3 — PayG: Customers ab apne hisaab se recharge karenge — monthly plans nahi. Daily, weekly, jitna chahein. Aapka kaam: 30-day ISP recharge ensure karein.

Final: "Samajh gaya" — must tap. App unlocks.
For partners who saw contextual on Day 1 (S1): The blocker is warm — they already have a piece. Now they get the full picture. The contextual was the appetiser, this is the main course.

For partners who didn't open on Day 1 (S0): The blocker is their first touch. It's cold but necessary — they've had zero exposure, and we can't let them use the app without knowing the world changed.
State transition: S0/S1 → S2 (blocker completed)
After blocker: App unlocks. Partner can now work (tickets, wallet, etc.). Contextual section popups now fire as they navigate — these add depth to the headlines from the blocker.

Post-blocker contextual popups (only for S2 partners):

Now that the partner has the full headline picture, section popups add detail:

Wallet: "Naye system mein withdrawal rules badlenge — details naye app mein milegi."
Tickets: "Aap jo ticket solve kar rahe hain — iska directly bonus pe asar padega. 5 quality factors track honge."
Asset/NetBox: "Aapke devices ka pura hisaab naye app mein dikhega — har device separately tracked hogi."

These fire one-time per section. Only visible to partners who have completed the blocker (S2+). If partner is still S0/S1, they don't see these — they see the blocker first.
3
07/04

WA Nudge for Non-Completers + Protection for Completers

Two different actions based on partner state
Partner stateWhat happens on Day 3
S0 — Not opened WA nudge: "Wiom mein bada badlaav aa raha hai — aapne abhi tak update nahi dekha. App kholein — zaruri hai."

This is the second WA (first was Day 1). Stronger language. Still a redirect — education is in the app.
S1 — Saw contextual only WA nudge: "Aapne update ka ek hissa dekha — puri picture dekhein. App kholein."

They saw the teaser but haven't hit the blocker. Nudge them to open app → blocker fires.
S2 — Blocker done Protection message (WA + app popup):
"No enforcement until 01/05. Samajhne aur adjust karne ka time hai. Trust Line: [number]"

Reassurance after the heavy education. Anxiety relief.
S3+ — Sections seen Same protection message + contextual popups continue in unvisited sections.
Day 3 is the first state-split. Non-completers get pushed. Completers get reassured. Same day, different experience based on data.
4
08/04

Video + Quiz for Completers, Escalation for Non-Completers

Deepen understanding for those ahead, push those behind
Partner stateWhat happens on Day 4
S2/S3 — Blocker done Video surfaced: App open popup — "Pura system detail mein samjhein — video dekhein, Paathshala mein."

2-3 min video: work + earning + PayG as one coherent story. Everything they've seen in fragments, now connected.

WA: "Wiom Paathshala mein video dekhein — quiz bhi karein."
S4 — Video watched Quiz surfaced: "Video dekh liya? Ab quiz karein — 3 sawaal."

Same quiz format that worked for PayG. 3-4 comprehension questions. Must pass.

This is the confirmation gate — we now know they know.
S0/S1 — Still haven't completed blocker No WA on Day 4 (3 WAs in 4 days = spam). But the blocker is still waiting. When they open app, blocker fires immediately.

Contextual popups are suppressed for non-completers — blocker first.
The beautiful weave:
Day 1: "Something is changing" (contextual teaser) →
Day 2: "Here's what changed" (blocker, full picture) →
Day 3: "Don't worry, you have time" (protection) →
Day 4: "Now understand it deeply" (video) → "Prove you understood" (quiz)

Each step builds on the last. Nothing is disconnected.
5
09/04

App Transition + Forced Blocker for Holdouts

Everyone gets download instructions. Non-completers hit the wall.
Partner stateWhat happens on Day 5
S2+ — Blocker done (any level) App transition: Popup + WA + SMS — "Naya app kal launch. Download karein: [link]. Old app kal band hoga."

For S5 (quiz passed): They're fully educated. Just the practical instruction. For S2/S3 (blocker done, may not have seen video/quiz): Transition instruction + Paathshala nudge.
S0/S1 — STILL haven't completed blocker App is now locked. Full-screen message:

"Wiom ka system badal chuka hai. Aap abhi tak update nahi dekha hai."

Aapko yeh samajhna zaruri hai — neeche tap karein aur complete karein.

[Abhi Complete Karein →]

This is not the soft blocker. This is the hard lock. App is unusable until they complete.
WA (final): "Kal se naya app. Aapne abhi tak update nahi dekha — app kholein aur complete karein. [link]"

Third and final WA. Maximum urgency.
Why Day 5 is the wall: Tomorrow the new app launches. A partner who enters the new app without ANY education is the worst-case scenario — they see unfamiliar numbers, call Trust Line, or disengage. Day 5 is the last chance to prevent that. The hard lock is justified here — it's 1 day before launch.
🚀

Launch — New App Live, Old App Blocked

Declaration becomes reality. Every partner now on the new app.

Summary — The Weave

Day 1: "Something is changing" — contextual, in the section you're already in. Appetiser.

Day 2: "Here's what changed" — blocker, full picture, must acknowledge. Main course.

Day 2+: "Here's the detail for your section" — contextual popups unlock after blocker. Side dishes, only in sections you visit.

Day 3: "You have time" — protection for completers. Push for non-completers.

Day 4: "Now understand it deeply" — video. Then "prove you understood" — quiz. Dessert.

Day 5: "Get the new app" — practical transition. Non-completers hit the wall.

Day 6: New app live. Declaration becomes reality. Individual numbers visible.

Nothing is disconnected. Everything builds. Every partner experiences it in the right order based on their own behaviour — not our calendar.
