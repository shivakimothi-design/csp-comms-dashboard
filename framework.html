CSP Migration — Objective & Framework

Foundation document  |  ~1,200 CSPs  |  Announcement: 05/04/26  |  App Live: 10/04/26

Objective

By 10/04/26 (app live), every CSP has a mental model of what changed and why.
By 30/04/26 (month end), every CSP trusts the system — no unexplained number, no surprise deduction, no confusion call.

Specifically, by the time the new app goes live:

  1. Knows what changed — earning structure, work allocation, PayG, interface
  2. Is not surprised by anything they see in the new app on day 1
  3. Has the new app installed and can open it

And by month end:

  1. Understands their individual numbers — bonus, devices, quality score, payout breakdown
  2. Knows how to improve — what helps/hurts their score, how to earn more
  3. Self-serves from the app — does not need to call Trust Line for information that's available in-app

Governing Rules

✔ Only introduce what becomes relevant that day If the partner can't act on it or see it yet, don't tell them about it. Information without context creates confusion, not clarity.
✘ Never repeat what was already shown If a concept was covered on Day 1, don't re-explain it on Day 3. Build on what was said — don't restart.
✔ Maximum 1 blocker in the entire sequence One forced acknowledgment (cannot skip). Use only when CSP must understand something before taking any action. Every other touchpoint is contextual.
✘ Never show what CSP cannot act on yet Don't show device counts before app is live (old app can't show CSP-specific data). Don't show quality score before measurement starts. Don't show projected bonus before the screen exists.
✔ Old app = declaration. New app = personalisation. Old app educates on rules and structure (same for all). New app shows individual reality (different for each CSP).
✘ WhatsApp is never the education WA gets the partner to the right place (old app, new app, video). It doesn't carry the educational content itself.

Blocker vs Contextual Framework

TypeWhen to useRules
BLOCKER CSP must acknowledge before they can take any action in app. They cannot dismiss or skip. Maximum 1 in entire sequence. Day 1 of announcement (05/04/26) only. Covers the "what changed" — not the details.
CONTEXTUAL Available, not forced. CSP can access anytime. System surfaces it based on state or time. All subsequent days. Popup triggered by partner action (opening ticket, visiting homepage). Dismissable. Available in Wiom Paathshala permanently.
Why only 1 blocker: Every blocker is friction. Friction on Day 1 is acceptable — the partner needs to know the world has changed. Friction on Day 2, 3, 4 is not — they have work to do (tickets). After the blocker, all education is contextual: surfaced when relevant, dismissable, and always revisitable.

Migration Phases

Phase 0 — Pre-Announcement
Operational Only
Before 05/04/26

Only operational comms that don't reference the new system. PNM telemetry installation is the one active workstream — partner confirms technician visit. No mention of bonus, earning changes, or new app.

Why no system mention yet: If we tell partners "the system is changing" 10 days before it goes live, but they can't see or do anything about it, we create anxiety without resolution. Telemetry installation is operational — it doesn't require context about the new system.
Phase 1 — Announcement to Launch
Declaration Mode — Build the Mental Model
05/04/26 – 09/04/26 (5 days)

Old app capability: Popup on trigger, static content only.
Mode: Declaration. Rules and structure. Same for all CSPs. No individual data.
Goal: By 10/04/26, partner knows what changed and is not surprised when they open the new app.

Day 1
05/04/26
The Announcement BLOCKER
What changed and why — the three shifts explained:
1. Work: Connections come from system, mandatory, no manual intervention, app is essential.
2. Earning: ₹300/connection stays. Bonus now one monthly amount (up to ₹120) based on quality. NetBox: ₹2/day idle fee, ₹50 collection, ₹200 security deposit.
3. PayG: Customers recharge as needed (not monthly). Your job: ensure 30-day ISP recharge.

Format: Old app popup (blocker — must acknowledge). WA + SMS redirect to old app. Video + quiz recommended.
This is the only blocker in the entire sequence.
Day 2
06/04/26
Carry Fee Policy CONTEXTUAL
Idle NetBox = ₹2/day. Device-wise details will be visible in new app.

Format: WA declaration. Short, factual. No individual device counts (old app can't show them).
Why today: Earning structure was introduced yesterday. Now the specific policy that affects their devices.
Day 3
07/04/26
Protection Confirmation CONTEXTUAL
You'll have time to adjust. No enforcement until 01/05/26. Focus on understanding first.

Format: WA text. Psychological safety net.
Why today: After 2 days of "what changed", partner may feel anxious. This says: breathe, you have time.
Day 4-5
08-09/04/26
App Transition Instructions CONTEXTUAL
New app is coming tomorrow/today. Download link / update instructions. Delete old app if applicable.

Format: WA + SMS (deep link) + Old app redirect screen.
Why now: Practical instruction right before launch. Not earlier — too early creates "download and wait" confusion.
What is NOT said in Phase 1: No individual device counts (can't show on old app). No quality score details (measurement hasn't started). No bonus projections (no data yet). No "open app to see your state" (new app isn't live yet). Only rules, structure, and timeline.
Phase 2 — Launch Day
Personalisation Starts — CSP Meets Their Reality
10/04/26

New app goes live. Old app blocked — redirects to new app.
Mode: Personalised. CSP sees their individual data for the first time.
Goal: Partner is on the new app, sees their state, and starts working (tickets).

Launch
10/04/26
App is Live + First Open Experience
WA: "Naya app live hai" with deep link to open app. Trust Line number.
Old app: Blocked. Opens to "yeh app band hai — naya app kholein" redirect.
New app first open: Popup/overlay showing:
  • Their earning structure (rate card — now with real numbers tied to their account)
  • Their device status (active vs idle — first time seeing individual counts)
  • How the app works — tickets, earnings, devices all live here now
After popup dismissed: regular dashboard with tickets on top.

This is the moment declaration becomes reality. Everything said in Phase 1 now has numbers attached.
The shift: Phase 1 said "idle devices will cost ₹2/day." Phase 2 shows "you have 2 idle devices — ₹2/day is accruing." Same rule, now personal. This is why we didn't show device counts earlier — old app couldn't, and showing wrong numbers destroys trust faster than showing no numbers.
Phase 3 — Post-Launch
Trigger-Based — Respond to Partner's Reality
Day 3 post-launch – 30/04/26

Mode: Trigger-based, not calendar-based. Each message fires on a condition — not a date.
Goal: Partner understands their individual performance, takes action, and sees their first bonus with full context.

Day 3-5
~13-15/04
Telemetry → Bonus Connection
Trigger: Telemetry status = Pending only.
Partner now knows what bonus is (from Phase 1 + launch day rate card). Now connect: "telemetry installation required for bonus eligibility."
Why now and not earlier: the consequence has meaning only after the context exists.
Day 7-10
~17-20/04
Quality Score Check
Trigger: First bonus cycle is active.
"Check your quality score — see what's helping and hurting." Multi-channel: WA + in-app banner + push for inactive.
Product dependency: quality score screen must be live.
Day 14
~24/04
Protection Close-out
Trigger: Calendar — all partners.
"Enforcement starts 01/05. This is the time to adjust." Closes the Phase 1 protection promise with a specific date.
Day 20-22
~30/04-02/05
Pre-Bonus Nudge
Trigger: Bonus cycle closing window.
"Cycle closing — check projected bonus. Last chance to fix issues."
Product dependency: projected bonus screen must be live. Most important post-launch message.
Month End
~01/05/26+
Bonus Credited
Trigger: System event — bonus calculated.
Full itemised breakdown: connections, bonus tier, collections, deductions, total. If not paid: reason shown explicitly.
Non-negotiable: every rupee accounted for. No lump sums.
What is NOT in Phase 3: No re-education on "what changed" (Phase 1 did that). No re-explanation of rate card (it's permanently in the app). Phase 3 is purely about the partner's individual reality — their score, their bonus, their actions.
Ongoing — Contextual Education
Behaviour-Triggered, Always Available
Continuous

Education doesn't end at month-end. But it's never calendar-blasted again. It surfaces based on what the partner does or doesn't do.

TriggerWhat surfaces
Quality score dropsSpecific tips tied to their data — "2 idle devices, 1 open issue"
Performing wellReinforcement — "connections stable, issues resolved on time"
After bonus (below expectation)Improvement tips tied to the specific reason for low bonus
Before enforcement (01/05)At-risk partners get actionable tips on compliance screen
Partner visits PaathshalaAll education modules, videos, rate card, guides — always available
